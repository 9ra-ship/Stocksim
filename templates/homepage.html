Your Questions, Answered

Find everything you need to know about Stock Master, from learning to supported assets.

What is Stock Master? +
Stock Master is a virtual trading platform designed to help beginners learn stock market investing without risking real money. You get a virtual portfolio to practice trading with real-time market data.
How fast are transactions? +
All trades execute instantly at current market prices, just like paper trading. There's no settlement period since you're using virtual funds.
Is trading on Stock Master secure? +
Absolutely. We use bank-level encryption for all data, and since you're not using real money, there's no financial risk. Your personal information is always protected.
Do I need to verify my identity? +
No identity verification is required as this is a learning platform using virtual currency. Just sign up with your email to get started.
Which cryptocurrencies and stocks are supported? +
We support all major NYSE and NASDAQ stocks, plus top cryptocurrencies including Bitcoin, Ethereum, Solana, XRP, and Dash. New assets are added regularly.
Can I access Stock Master without being associated with an institution? +
Yes! Stock Master is available to anyone worldwide. No institutional affiliation or prior trading experience is required - it's perfect for individual learners.
What are the requirements for transactions? +
There are no minimum requirements. You'll start with a virtual balance to begin trading immediately. All transactions use our virtual currency.
How can I contact support? +
Our support team is available 24/7 via email at support@stockmaster.com or through the live chat feature in the platform. Average response time is under 2 hours.
